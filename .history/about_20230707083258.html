About

Who We Are?

The Students Loan is a private student loan non-
profit program that provides No-interest loans,
sometimes called scholarship loans or interest-free
loans and scholarships, which are awarded to
eligible students that do not accrue interest at all.
Get started by checking your loan eligibility in just
10 seconds.

What We Offer

Providing interest-free loans and scholarships for post-
secondary and undergraduate education to students based
on financial need has been the core function of The Student
loan Hub since its creation over some decades now.

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is to be the Hub that best recognizes and
serves the needs of Nigerian students. We strive to
provide students world-class resources to help them
investigate and pursue their education, through
relevant content, custom online tools, and engaging
websites that offer only best-in-class products and services.
